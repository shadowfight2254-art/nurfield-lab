Privacy Policy
LAST UPDATED: JANUARY 2025

WHAT WE COLLECT

We collect your name and email address through Google OAuth authentication. We also store the research content you create: nodes, experiments, uploaded documents, and session history. This data is necessary to power your personal research workspace.

HOW WE USE IT

Your data is used solely to power your personal research workspace and AI interactions. Your research content is used to calibrate the AI collaborator specifically to your framework and context.

DATA SHARING

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party. Your research is yours alone. The only external service that receives any data is the AI provider (Anthropic) for processing your queries — no identifying information is transmitted.

UPLOADED DOCUMENTS

Your uploaded research documents are stored securely and are never used to train any AI model. They are stored exclusively for your personal use within NurField Lab.

DATA DELETION

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, all your data including research nodes, experiments, documents, and session history will be permanently deleted.

SECURITY

We use industry standard encryption for all stored data. Authentication is handled by Google's secure OAuth 2.0 system.

COOKIES

Cookies are used only for session management and authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies of any kind.

CONTACT

For privacy concerns or data deletion requests, contact the site owner directly.
